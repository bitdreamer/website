Here are some pictures from putting in the stereo system.
 

The stereo system is a couple of ordinary DLP projectors and a polarization-preserving screen.  We don't really use glasses left-eye and right-eye on the projectors, but this makes obvious how each projector is polarized for one eye.  To drive it we have two headed video cards.  We do not have a splitter for a stereo DVD yet.
