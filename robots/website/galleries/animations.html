All animations are in .avi format and should play in most IE versions and probably Netscape as well. They should also play in Windows Media Player along with other media players. If you want to save the clip, right click on the image and choose "Save Target As..." not "Save Picture As..." as that will only save the image and not the video clip. NOTE: Sometimes after playing a clip in the web browser, it'll stubborn about going to another page. Try refreshing then clicking on another link.


In the Table Animation you can see the RCX bump into a box, back up and turn, then move toward the edge of the table but stop, back up and turn away from the edge!
In the Maze Animation you can see the RCX maneuver around a 90 degree left turn of the reflective tape maze!
In the Thrower Animation you can see the RCX ram into a trash can and attack it by throwing a green gear at it!
