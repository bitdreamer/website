Dr. Barry Koster, a Computer Science professor at Meredith College, and Rebecca Fosdick, Class of 2006 at Meredith College, began working on the "Robots in Class" Undergraduate Research project in the summer of 2003. Working with the LEGO Mindstorms robots, their main objective is to enhance the teaching of Meredith's programming sequence by coming up with lab assignments for CSC1 students.

Feel free to wander about this site to learn more about their research and how you could do something similar!
